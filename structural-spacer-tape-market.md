<p><p>The Structural Spacer Tape market includes two main types: double-sided and single-sided tapes. Double-sided tapes feature adhesive on both sides, providing enhanced bonding and stability in structural applications, ideal for securing components without visible fasteners. Single-sided tapes have adhesive on only one side, used primarily for mounting or insulation purposes. Both types serve various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and construction, where they offer strength, durability, and ease of installation in structural assemblies.</p></p>
