<p><ul><li>Venous Blood Collection</li><li>Capillary Blood Collection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vacutainer market encompasses applications in both venous and capillary blood collection. Venous blood collection involves drawing blood from a vein using a needle and a Vacutainer tube, ensuring precise sample collection for laboratory testing. Capillary blood collection, on the other hand, is performed using fingerstick or heelstick techniques, suitable for patients with difficult venous access or for point-of-care testing. Both methods utilize specialized Vacutainer systems designed to maintain sample integrity and facilitate efficient laboratory workflows.</p></p>
