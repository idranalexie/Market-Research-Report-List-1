<p><p>The nitrogen gas booster market is categorized into two primary types: single-stage pumps and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed to compress nitrogen gas in one step, making them suitable for applications requiring lower pressures and volumes. In contrast, multi-stage pumps utilize multiple compression stages to achieve higher pressures and are ideal for industrial applications needing greater efficiency and pressure control. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including aerospace, food processing, and chemical manufacturing, supporting diverse operational needs.</p></p>
