<p><ul><li>Paraffinic Lubricant</li><li>Naphtenic Lubricant</li><li>Aromatic Lubricant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive mineral fluid lubricants market encompasses various types, including paraffinic, naphthenic, and aromatic lubricants. Paraffinic lubricants are derived from refined crude oil and are known for their stability and good low-temperature properties. Naphthenic lubricants, characterized by their cyclic structures, offer excellent thermal stability and are often used in applications requiring high performance. Aromatic lubricants, containing aromatic hydrocarbons, provide effective lubrication but may have lower thermal stability. Each type serves specific needs within the automotive industry for optimal engine performance.</p></p>
