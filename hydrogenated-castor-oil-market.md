PEG-40 offers excellent solubilizing properties and is commonly used in cosmetic formulations. PEG-60 provides enhanced emulsification and stability, making it suitable for creams and lotions. PEG-80 is more viscous and serves as a thickening agent in personal care products. These variations enable tailored applications across cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food industries.</p></p>
