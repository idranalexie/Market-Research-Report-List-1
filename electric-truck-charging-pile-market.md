<p><p>The electric truck charging pile market primarily consists of two types: DC charging piles and AC charging piles. DC charging piles provide rapid charging by converting alternating current (AC) from the grid to direct current (DC), allowing for faster energy transfer and shorter charging times, making them suitable for long-haul electric trucks. In contrast, AC charging piles supply power directly in AC form, typically used for home or workplace charging, which is generally slower but more cost-effective for regular use.</p></p>
