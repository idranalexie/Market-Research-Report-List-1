<p><p>The sterile empty vials market includes various types designed for pharmaceutical and laboratory use. Sterile evacuated vials are pre-evacuated to maintain sterile conditions and are ideal for vacuum-sealed storage. Sterile nitrogen-filled vials contain nitrogen gas to prevent oxidation and preserve sensitive substances, making them suitable for reactive compounds. Sterile air-filled vials are filled with air and are used for less reactive substances. Each type serves specific applications, ensuring product stability and integrity in different environments.</p></p>
