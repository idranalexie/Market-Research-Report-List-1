<p><p>The Hard Tag Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) System is a security technology used predominantly in retail environments to prevent theft and loss of merchandise. <p><p>Hard Tag Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) Systems encompass several market types, each utilizing different technologies to prevent theft. </p><p>Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) systems employ tags and readers to track items. Acoustic Magnetic (AM) Anti-Theft systems use magnetic fields to detect tagged items. Radio Frequency (RF) systems operate on a specific radio frequency to sense tags. Electromagnetic Wave (EM) Anti-Theft systems generate electromagnetic fields to capture tag signals. Additionally, "Other" market segments include emerging technologies and hybrid systems that combine these methods for enhanced security.</p></p>
