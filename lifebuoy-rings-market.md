<p><p>The lifebuoy rings market consists of two primary types: inherent buoyant lifebuoys and inflatable lifebuoys. Inherent buoyant lifebuoys are made from solid, buoyant materials that maintain their shape and stability in water, making them reliable for immediate deployment. In contrast, inflatable lifebuoys are lightweight and compact, designed to be inflated quickly when needed, promoting ease of storage and transport. Both types serve essential roles in water safety, catering to different operational needs and environments in maritime and recreational activities.</p></p>
