<p><p>The diabetes injection pens market comprises two main types: reusable insulin pens and disposable insulin pens. Reusable insulin pens are designed for multiple uses, allowing patients to replace the insulin cartridge while reusing the pen body, which promotes sustainability and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, disposable insulin pens are single-use devices pre-filled with insulin, eliminating the need for cartridge changes and offering convenience. Both types aim to enhance patient adherence and ease of use in managing diabetes effectively.</p></p>
