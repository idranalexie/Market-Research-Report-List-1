<p><strong>The Transmission Towers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hanging Tower</li><li>Resisting-Tensile Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transmission towers market can be categorized into hanging towers and resisting-tensile towers. Hanging towers utilize a suspension system, facilitating the support of overhead power lines, ideal for covering large spans with minimal ground disturbance. In contrast, resisting-tensile towers are designed to handle significant mechanical stresses and are often used in areas with challenging terrains. Each type serves distinct infrastructural needs while ensuring the efficient transmission of electricity, contributing to the overall reliability of power distribution networks.</p></p>
