<p><p>The medical MRI equipment market is categorized into three main types: Magnet systems, RF (Radio Frequency) systems, and Computer imaging systems. Magnet systems are essential for generating strong magnetic fields required for imaging, while RF systems facilitate the transmission and reception of radio waves, crucial for signal generation. Computer imaging systems process the data acquired, transforming it into detailed images for diagnostic purposes. This segmentation highlights the diverse technologies that work in concert to provide advanced medical imaging capabilities.</p></p>
