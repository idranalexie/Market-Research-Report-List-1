<p><p>The water-based gravure ink market includes primarily two types: photo gravure ink and intaglio gravure ink. Photo gravure ink is designed for high-quality printing applications, utilizing photographic processes for detailed imagery, making it ideal for packaging and labels. Intaglio gravure ink, on the other hand, is primarily used for fine art and specialty printing, characterized by deeper ink penetration and vibrant color reproduction. Both types leverage eco-friendly water-based formulations, promoting safer printing practices while maintaining superior print quality.</p></p>
