<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger car tire market is primarily divided into two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature better flexibility, improved fuel efficiency, and enhanced traction, making them the preferred choice for modern vehicles. They have steel belts that enhance stability and longevity. In contrast, bias tires have a construction that allows for greater sidewall strength, offering better handling and load capacity, but typically have a shorter lifespan and less fuel efficiency. Each type caters to different driving needs and preferences.</p></p>
