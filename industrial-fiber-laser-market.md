<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial fiber laser market features two primary types: Continuous Wave (CW) fiber lasers and pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers emit a constant beam of light, making them ideal for applications requiring high power and precision, such as cutting and welding. In contrast, pulsed fiber lasers generate short bursts of energy, allowing for improved control in processes like marking and engraving, where material impact needs to be managed carefully. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
