<p><p>The prostate cancer biomarker market comprises three main types: urine, blood, and serum biomarkers. Urine biomarkers are used for non-invasive screening and monitoring, detecting genetic mutations and protein markers associated with prostate cancer. Blood biomarkers primarily identify circulating tumor cells and specific proteins indicative of cancer presence or progression. Serum biomarkers focus on measuring specific substances in serum that can suggest cancer presence, aiding in diagnosis and prognosis. Together, these biomarkers enhance early detection and personalized treatment strategies for prostate cancer.</p></p>
