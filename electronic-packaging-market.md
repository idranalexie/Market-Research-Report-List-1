<p><p>The electronic packaging market encompasses various types, including organic substrates, bonding wires, ceramic packages, and others. Organic substrates, often made of epoxy or polyimide, are key for interconnections in electronic devices due to their lightweight and flexible properties. Bonding wires, typically gold or aluminum, provide crucial electrical connections between the chip and the substrate. Ceramic packages offer high thermal stability and electrical insulation, suitable for high-performance applications. Other market types include advanced packaging techniques like 3D packaging and system-in-package solutions.</p></p>
