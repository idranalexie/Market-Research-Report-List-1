<p><p>The washbasin mixer tap market includes various types based on their thread sizes, primarily G1/2, G3/4, and G3/8. G1/2 taps have a diameter of half an inch and are commonly used in standard installations. G3/4 taps are three-quarters of an inch, suitable for applications requiring higher flow rates. G3/8 taps, measuring three-eighths of an inch, are typically used in compact spaces where size constraints exist. Each type serves specific plumbing needs, influencing design and installation choices.</p></p>
