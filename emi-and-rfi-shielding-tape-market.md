<p><p>The EMI and RFI shielding tape market is categorized primarily into two types: metal foil and conductive polymers. Metal foil tapes, typically made from copper, aluminum, or nickel, provide superior shielding effectiveness against electromagnetic interference, making them ideal for applications requiring high signal integrity. Conductive polymer tapes, made from plastics infused with conductive materials, offer flexibility and lightweight properties, suitable for applications with complex geometries. Both types are essential for protecting sensitive electronic equipment from interference and ensuring optimal performance.</p></p>
