<p><ul><li>Dry-type Padmount Transformers</li><li>Liquid-immersed Padmount Transformers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Padmount transformers are essential for electrical distribution, and they come in two main types: dry-type and liquid-immersed. Dry-type padmount transformers use air as a cooling medium, making them suitable for indoor applications and environments where safety is a priority. They require less maintenance and are environmentally friendly. Conversely, liquid-immersed padmount transformers utilize oil for cooling, offering higher efficiency and greater load capacities, making them ideal for outdoor installations and applications that demand robust performance in varying conditions.</p></p>
