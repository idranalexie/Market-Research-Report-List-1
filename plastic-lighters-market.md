<p><ul><li>Flint Type</li><li>Electronic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic lighters market is primarily divided into two types: flint-type and electronic-type lighters. Flint-type lighters use a striking mechanism to ignite, featuring a wheel that creates a spark when turned against a flint. They are widely popular due to their affordability and reliability. In contrast, electronic-type lighters utilize a battery-powered ignition system, often employing plasma technology or an electric arc. These lighters are rechargeable, eco-friendly, and preferred for their safety and ease of use, appealing to a modern consumer base.</p></p>
