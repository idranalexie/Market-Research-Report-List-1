<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multiple Stages</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water booster pump market is categorized into single-stage and multi-stage types. Single-stage pumps consist of one impeller and are suitable for applications requiring moderate pressure and flow, making them ideal for residential use. Multi-stage pumps, on the other hand, feature multiple impellers, allowing them to generate higher pressures and are often utilized in industrial settings or high-rise buildings. Both types cater to varying needs, from domestic water supply to complex irrigation systems and fire protection applications.</p></p>
