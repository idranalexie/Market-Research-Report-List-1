<p><p>The medical stents market encompasses various types designed for specific anatomical locations and medical conditions. Peripheral stents are used to open narrowed arteries in the limbs. Biliary stents alleviate bile duct obstructions. Intracranial stents support blood vessels in the brain. Vena cava stents address blood flow issues in the vena cava. Pancreatic stents help treat pancreatitis-related conditions.</p></p>
